About Me

I combine my Bachelor of Commerce education with insights from diverse competitive experiences to identify opportunities and analyze challenges from multiple angles. My background in competitive sports, e-sports, and international problem-solving competitions has built my resilience under pressure and adaptability to changing circumstances.

Through my fashion brand concept project, I explored market research, trademark registration processes, and supply chain considerations. This hands-on project strengthened my understanding of both analytical and creative aspects of business development.

My experience in high-volume service environments has sharpened my teamwork skills and ability to maintain high standards in fast-paced settings. Combined with my classical guitar training (Grade 6 AMEB) and documentary filmmaking experience, I bring a multifaceted perspective that balances quantitative analysis with qualitative understanding.

I aim to begin my career in consulting or risk management at an established firm like one of the Big 4, where I can apply my analytical skills while developing strategic insights that will support my longer-term entrepreneurial aspirations.
