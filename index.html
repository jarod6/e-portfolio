About Me

As a recent Bachelor of Commerce graduate, I combine analytical thinking with an entrepreneurial mindset to approach business challenges from multiple perspectives. My background in competitive sports, e-sports, and international problem-solving competitions has built my resilience under pressure and adaptability to changing circumstances.

During my academic journey, I've cultivated strong analytical capabilities while maintaining a forward-thinking approach to identifying opportunities and innovative solutions. My experience with my fashion brand concept development taught me valuable lessons about market research, trademark registration, and supply chain considerations.

My time working in a busy bar/restaurant environment honed my teamwork skills and ability to maintain high standards in fast-paced settings. Combined with my classical guitar training (Grade 6 AMEB) and experience in documentary-style surf filmmaking, I bring a unique blend of creative and analytical perspectives to my work.

I'm particularly drawn to consulting and risk management roles within established organizations like the Big 4, where I can apply my analytical capabilities while developing the strategic insights that will support my longer-term entrepreneurial aspirations.
